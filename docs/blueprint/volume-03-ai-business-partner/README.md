## Purpose
Define the intelligence layer of Project WORLD. This volume is the enterprise architecture and functional specification for the AI Business Partner. Every future AI capability, AI agent, reasoning engine, planning engine, recommendation engine, automation engine, and business advisor must conform to the concepts defined here.

## Scope
Functional and architectural specification of how the AI Business Partner should think, collaborate, reason, and support founders and organizations. This is not an implementation guide; technology design lives in Part C of the Master Blueprint. Concepts build on [Volume 01 - Vision & Philosophy](/docs/blueprint/volume-01-vision-and-philosophy/README.md) and [Volume 02 - Business Foundation](/docs/blueprint/volume-02-business-foundation/README.md).

## How to Use This Volume
Each chapter is a standalone specification with consistent metadata, first-principles explanation, tables, Mermaid diagrams and decision flows where useful, enterprise examples, and cross-references. Terminology is consistent across the volume and aligned with the AI Glossary and AI Terminology appendices.
