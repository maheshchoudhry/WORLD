## Purpose
Define how WORLD understands businesses, analyzes information, discovers problems, identifies opportunities, evaluates risks, supports strategic thinking, and generates explainable recommendations. This volume is the intelligence framework connecting the Business Foundation (Volume 02) with the AI Business Partner (Volume 03). Every future dashboard, analytics engine, AI recommendation, forecasting engine, planning engine, automation engine, and executive assistant must follow the principles defined here.

## Scope
An enterprise Business Intelligence and Decision Science handbook: intelligence foundation, business analysis, problem solving, strategic intelligence, planning and forecasting, decision frameworks, performance intelligence, and enterprise intelligence, plus practical appendices. It defines concepts, frameworks, and how WORLD applies them; detailed technology design belongs to Part C.

## How to Use This Volume
Each chapter explains why the concept exists, where it is used, how WORLD implements it, and its relationship with the AI Business Partner, the ERP layer, and the Business Foundation. Chapters use tables, Mermaid diagrams and decision trees, framework illustrations, examples, and cross-references. Terminology is consistent and aligned with the Volume 02 and Volume 03 glossaries.
