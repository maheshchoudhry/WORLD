## Purpose

This appendix provides balanced-scorecard templates that translate strategy into a coherent set of measures across four perspectives. The scorecard gives the AI Business Partner and leadership a single, balanced view of performance that avoids over-optimizing any one dimension.

## Scope

Covers the four Balanced Scorecard perspectives - Financial, Customer, Internal Process, and Learning & Growth - with example metrics, target columns, and a consolidated summary. Replace example metrics and targets with organization-specific values at use time.

## How to use

For each perspective, list objectives, the measures that indicate progress, targets, actuals for the period, and status. Keep the total number of measures manageable (typically 4-7 per perspective) to preserve focus.

Status legend: On = within tolerance; At-risk = within warning band; Off = threshold breached.
