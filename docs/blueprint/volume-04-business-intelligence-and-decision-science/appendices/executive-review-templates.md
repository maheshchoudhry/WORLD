## Purpose

This appendix provides standardized agendas and structures for the recurring executive review cadences in the WORLD operating rhythm. Consistent templates make reviews efficient, comparable across periods, and easy for the AI Business Partner to prepare and summarize.

## Scope

Covers four review types: the Quarterly Business Review (QBR), the Monthly Business Review (MBR), the KPI Review, and the Decision Review. Each template is fill-in-ready with recommended timeboxes.

## 1. Quarterly Business Review (QBR)

Suggested duration: 90-120 minutes.

| Section | Timebox | Content |
|---|---|---|
| Quarter summary | 10 min | Headline results vs. plan; theme of the quarter. |
| Strategic objectives (OKRs) | 20 min | Progress per objective; on-track / at-risk / off-track. |
| Financial performance | 15 min | Revenue, margin, cash, key variances. |
| Customer & market | 15 min | Acquisition, retention, NPS, competitive shifts. |
| Operations & delivery | 10 min | Throughput, quality, key incidents. |
| Risks & issues | 10 min | Top risks, mitigation status. |
| Priorities next quarter | 15 min | Committed objectives and resource asks. |
| Decisions & actions | 10 min | Decisions made; owners and due dates. |

**Record:** decisions log, action register (owner, due date), and next-quarter commitments.

## 2. Monthly Business Review (MBR)

Suggested duration: 60 minutes.

| Section | Timebox | Content |
|---|---|---|
| Scorecard snapshot | 10 min | Actual vs. target for the month across perspectives. |
| Financials | 10 min | P&L highlights, cash position, variance to budget. |
| Growth metrics | 10 min | Pipeline, bookings, churn, activation. |
| Operational health | 10 min | SLAs, quality, capacity. |
| Exceptions & escalations | 10 min | Items outside tolerance requiring decisions. |
| Actions | 10 min | Confirm owners and deadlines. |

**Rule:** discuss only exceptions and decisions; distribute the full data pack in advance.

## 3. KPI Review

Suggested duration: 30-45 minutes.

| KPI | Owner | Target | Actual | Status | Trend | Commentary / Action |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| `<KPI 1>` | `<name>` | `<target>` | `<actual>` | On/At-risk/Off | Up/Flat/Down | `<note>` |
| `<KPI 2>` | `<name>` | `<target>` | `<actual>` | On/At-risk/Off | Up/Flat/Down | `<note>` |
| `<KPI 3>` | `<name>` | `<target>` | `<actual>` | On/At-risk/Off | Up/Flat/Down | `<note>` |

Status legend: On = within tolerance; At-risk = within warning band; Off = breached threshold. Every Off item requires a named corrective action.

## 4. Decision Review

Used to review the quality and outcomes of significant past decisions (decision hygiene loop).

| Field | Content |
|---|---|
| Decision reviewed | `<Decision ID and statement>` |
| Date decided / reviewed | `<dates>` |
| Expected outcome | What was predicted at decision time. |
| Actual outcome | What happened. |
| Variance & causes | Gap between expected and actual, and why. |
| Decision quality | Was the process sound, independent of outcome? (Good process / Lucky / Unlucky / Poor process) |
| Lessons learned | What to repeat or change. |
| Follow-up actions | Owner and due date. |
