## Purpose
Define how WORLD is configured and applied for specific industries. Each industry solution maps the industry's business model, organization, and processes to the required ERP modules (Volume 06) and AI features (Volume 03), and specifies KPIs, compliance, dashboards, reporting, and a future roadmap. This volume makes WORLD's industry-independent core concretely applicable across verticals.

## Scope
Industry-level solution documentation for 18 industries organized into five sections, plus practical appendices. Industry solutions build on the Business Modules (Volume 06), the ERP Foundation (Volume 05), the AI Business Partner (Volume 03), Business Intelligence (Volume 04), and the Business Foundation (Volume 02).

## How to Use This Volume
Each industry chapter follows an identical enterprise template: Industry Overview, Business Model, Organization, Processes, Required ERP Modules, Required AI Features, KPIs, Compliance, Dashboards, Reporting, and Future Roadmap. Chapters use tables, Mermaid diagrams, and examples, cross-reference Volumes 02-06, and maintain consistent terminology.
