## Purpose

This appendix consolidates representative key performance indicators (KPIs) for each of the 18 industries in Volume 07 into a single reference. It gives industry leaders, solution owners, and analysts a consistent definition of the metrics that matter most in each vertical, complementing the module-level KPI catalog of Volume 06 with industry-specific measures.

## Scope

The catalog lists representative KPIs per industry with a plain-language definition. It is not exhaustive; individual industry chapters may define additional KPIs. Cross-industry operational metrics (for example, Inventory Turnover or On-Time Delivery) are defined once in the Volume 06 KPI Catalog and are referenced rather than repeated here. Each metric follows the single-definition principle to prevent conflicting interpretations.

## Reading the Catalog

| Column | Meaning |
|---|---|
| Metric | Canonical industry KPI name. |
| Definition | Plain-language meaning of the metric. |
| Industry | The industry for which the metric is most representative. |

## Section A - Production & Process Industries

| Metric | Definition | Industry |
|---|---|---|
| Milk Procurement Volume | Total quantity of raw milk collected over a period. | Dairy |
| Fat and SNF Yield | Recovery of fat and solids-not-fat from processed milk. | Dairy |
| Cold Chain Compliance Rate | Share of shipments held within temperature limits end to end. | Dairy |
| Overall Equipment Effectiveness (OEE) | Composite of equipment availability, performance, and quality. | Manufacturing |
| Scrap and Rework Rate | Share of output scrapped or reworked against total produced. | Manufacturing |
| Batch Right-First-Time Rate | Share of production batches meeting specification without deviation. | Food Processing |
| Yield Loss | Difference between theoretical and actual output from inputs. | Food Processing |
| Batch Release Cycle Time | Time from production completion to quality release of a batch. | Pharmaceutical |
| Deviation and CAPA Closure Rate | Share of quality deviations resolved within target time. | Pharmaceutical |
| Crop Yield per Hectare | Output quantity harvested per unit of cultivated area. | Agriculture |
| Post-Harvest Loss Rate | Share of harvested output lost before sale or processing. | Agriculture |

## Section B - Trade & Distribution

| Metric | Definition | Industry |
|---|---|---|
| Sales per Square Foot | Retail revenue generated per unit of selling area. | Retail |
| Shrinkage Rate | Share of inventory lost to theft, damage, or error. | Retail |
| Basket Size | Average number of items or value per customer transaction. | Retail |
| Order Fill Rate | Share of ordered quantity delivered from stock. | Wholesale |
| Days Sales Outstanding (DSO) | Average days to collect receivables after a sale. | Wholesale |
| Perfect Order Rate | Share of orders delivered complete, on time, and damage-free. | Distribution |
| Distribution Cost per Unit | Total distribution cost divided by units delivered. | Distribution |
| Service Absorption Rate | Share of dealership fixed costs covered by parts and service gross profit. | Automobile |
| Warranty Claim Rate | Share of units sold generating a warranty claim. | Automobile |

|---|---|---|

| Bed Occupancy Rate | Share of available beds occupied over a period. | Healthcare |
| Patient Readmission Rate | Share of patients readmitted within a defined window. | Healthcare |
| Student Enrolment Rate | Share of available places filled by enrolled students. | Education |
| Student Retention Rate | Share of students continuing across periods. | Education |
| Revenue per Available Room (RevPAR) | Room revenue divided by available rooms. | Hospitality |

| Realization Rate | Share of billed value collected against standard rates. | Professional Services |
