## Purpose

This appendix curates the established industry frameworks, standards, and bodies that inform the design of WORLD's industry solutions. It gives readers a grounded set of well-known references so that the industry documentation in Volume 07 can be read against widely accepted practice and real regulatory sources rather than proprietary invention.

## Scope

References are grouped by topic and describe real, established frameworks, standards, and issuing bodies relevant to the 18 industries. Entries name and summarize each reference; they are provided as conceptual references rather than as external hyperlinks, and the authoritative text is always the issuing organization's published source. Internal WORLD documents are linked in the Cross-References and References sections.
