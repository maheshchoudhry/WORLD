## Purpose
Define the complete enterprise security architecture of Project WORLD: security philosophy, zero-trust, identity and access, cryptography and secrets, layered security, endpoint and session security, threat detection and response, compliance and continuity, and security governance. This volume is the authoritative security specification that protects the AI Business Partner (Volume 03), the ERP (Volumes 05-06), and the technology stack (Volumes 08-11).

## Scope
Security foundations and zero-trust; identity and access; cryptography and secrets; layer-by-layer security (network, API, application, database, infrastructure, cloud, container); endpoint and session; threat and response; compliance and continuity; and governance and evolution, plus appendices. This volume consolidates and elevates the security concerns introduced across Volumes 08-11 into one coherent enterprise security architecture.

## How to Use This Volume
Each chapter follows an identical enterprise template: Purpose, Architecture, Implementation Strategy, Business Value, Relationship to AI, Relationship to ERP, Relationship to Infrastructure, and Future Expansion. Chapters use Mermaid diagrams, tables, and examples, cross-reference Volumes 03/05/08/09/10/11, and maintain consistent terminology.
