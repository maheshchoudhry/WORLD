## Purpose
State the purpose, mission, and vision of Project WORLD as fixed reference statements.

## Scope
The three foundational statements and brief explanation of each. These statements are authoritative and are not to be altered except by the Founder.

## Purpose
The purpose of WORLD is to make high-quality business thinking and execution continuously available to every founder, so that better decisions and faster improvement become the normal condition of running a business.

This purpose frames WORLD as an instrument of judgment and action, not merely of record-keeping. It applies regardless of a business's size, industry, or stage.

## Mission
To build the most intelligent AI Business Partner capable of helping founders think better, make better decisions, execute faster, and continuously improve their businesses.

The mission names four concrete outcomes - better thinking, better decisions, faster execution, and continuous improvement - which together define what the AI Business Partner must deliver.

## Vision
A world in which every business, regardless of size, operates with the support of an intelligent partner that understands its context, advances its goals, and grows more capable over time.

The vision describes the desired end state: intelligent partnership as a standard condition of running a business, available broadly rather than to a privileged few.
