## Purpose
Record the founder's vision for Project WORLD and the long-term ambition of the project.

## Scope
The founder's intent and the long-term end state. Excludes near-term roadmap and delivery plans, which are defined in Part E.

## Founder Vision
The founder, Mahesh Choudhary, envisions WORLD as a permanent, evolving partner for founders - a system that removes the isolation and overload of decision-making by providing competent, context-aware support at every stage of a business. The founder's intent is a system that grows in capability alongside the businesses it serves, and that remains disciplined, documented, and coherent as it scales.

Central to the founder's vision is rigor: WORLD is to be built as a world-class engineering effort, with the repository as its single source of truth and documentation preceding implementation at every step.

## Long-Term Vision
Over the long term, WORLD aims to become the default operating layer through which founders run and improve their businesses - understanding the whole business, coordinating its operations, and continuously advancing its goals.

The long-term ambition is durability and depth: a partner that accumulates understanding, maintains engineering and documentation rigor, and compounds value for the founder across the entire lifetime of the business. Growth in scope is expected, but it must never come at the cost of coherence, clarity, or trust.
