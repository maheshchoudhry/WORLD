## Summary
Project WORLD is an AI-Native Business Operating System whose primary product is an AI Business Partner. The project is founded by Mahesh Choudhary and developed as a long-term, versioned program of work in which the repository is the single source of truth.

The problem WORLD addresses is that existing business software records activity but does not help operators think, decide, or improve. Founders manage fragmented tools, incomplete information, and slow decision cycles without an integrated, intelligent partner. WORLD exists to close the gap between business data and business judgment.

The purpose of WORLD is to make high-quality business thinking and execution continuously available to every founder. Its mission is to build the most intelligent AI Business Partner capable of helping founders think better, make better decisions, execute faster, and continuously improve their businesses. Its vision is a world in which every business, regardless of size, operates with the support of an intelligent partner that understands its context and advances its goals.

WORLD is an AI Business Partner delivered as an operating system for the business. It is not merely an ERP, a CRM, an accounting package, or a chatbot; although it incorporates operational capabilities, its defining identity is intelligence applied to decisions and execution. Its philosophy centers on augmenting human judgment, treating the system as a single source of truth, and improving continuously. Success is defined by the quality of decisions and outcomes it enables, not by feature count.

This volume defines identity only. Product modules, architecture, engineering, and go-to-market strategy are defined in later volumes of the Master Blueprint.
