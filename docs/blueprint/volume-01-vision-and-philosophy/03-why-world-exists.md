## Purpose
State the reason Project WORLD exists and the enduring need it serves.

## Scope
The rationale for the project. Excludes product and market specifics.

## Rationale
WORLD exists to close the gap between business data and business judgment. Recording systems answer the question of what happened. WORLD is built to help answer what it means, what to do, and how to do it faster and better.

## Reasons for Existence
- To give every founder an always-available, competent partner in thinking and execution, independent of team size or budget.
- To convert fragmented data into coherent understanding and actionable guidance.
- To reduce the founder's cognitive load by holding context and synthesizing across domains.
- To shift businesses from reactive operation to proactive, informed decision-making.
- To retain and compound organizational knowledge so the business improves continuously.

## Durability of the Need
The need WORLD serves is durable: businesses will always require good judgment applied quickly under uncertainty. Tools that only record activity leave that need unmet. WORLD exists to meet it, and its relevance persists as long as businesses must decide and act.
