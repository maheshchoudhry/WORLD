## Overview
Project WORLD is an AI-Native Business Operating System. Its primary product is an AI Business Partner: an intelligent system that works alongside a founder to understand the business, surface insight, support decisions, and drive execution. The project is founded by Mahesh Choudhary and developed as a long-term, versioned program of work.

WORLD is AI-native in that intelligence is the organizing principle of the system rather than an added feature. Conventional business systems are built around records and workflows, with analytics or assistants layered on afterward. WORLD inverts this order: business context, reasoning, and partnership are foundational, and operational modules serve that intelligence.

## Source of Truth
The GitHub repository is the single source of truth for the project. All knowledge, architecture, specification, and implementation exist within the repository and are documented before implementation. No authoritative knowledge exists outside the repository.

## Execution Model
Development follows a frozen Master Blueprint of 26 volumes organized into five parts: Foundation, Product, Technology, Engineering, and Product Management. Volumes are executed one at a time, in the approved order. Volume 01 - Vision & Philosophy is the foundation of this sequence and defines the identity that constrains and guides every subsequent volume.

This volume is descriptive of identity and intent. It does not specify features, architecture, or delivery plans; those are governed by later volumes.
