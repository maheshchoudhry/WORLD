## Purpose
Define the core terms used throughout the Master Blueprint to ensure consistent, precise language across all volumes.

## Scope
Terminology essential to Volume 01 and the blueprint as a whole. Domain-specific terms are defined in their respective volumes and consolidated in the governance glossary.

## Definitions
| Term | Definition |
|---|---|
| AI Business Partner | An intelligent system that works alongside a founder to understand the business, support decisions, and drive execution. The primary product of Project WORLD. |
| AI-Native | A design approach in which intelligence is the foundational organizing principle of the system rather than an added capability. |
| Business Operating System | An integrated system through which a business is understood, operated, and improved within a single environment. |
| Founder | The primary user WORLD serves. In this project, also the originator and authority, Mahesh Choudhary. |
| Single Source of Truth | The principle that authoritative knowledge exists in one place - the repository and system - and nowhere else. |
| Master Blueprint | The frozen, authoritative 26-volume specification of Project WORLD. |
| Volume | A self-contained unit of the Master Blueprint covering one domain, built one at a time in approved order. |
| Context | The accumulated understanding of a specific business that WORLD uses to reason and recommend. |
| Execution Mode | The operating state in which volumes are built in the approved order, one at a time. |
| Part | A grouping of related volumes within the Master Blueprint (Foundation, Product, Technology, Engineering, Product Management). |
