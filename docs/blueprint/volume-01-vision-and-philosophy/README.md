# Volume 01 - Vision & Philosophy

## Purpose
Establish the complete identity of Project WORLD: the problem it addresses, the reason it exists, its purpose, mission, and vision, the definition of the product, the philosophy and principles that guide it, the founder and long-term vision, the definition of success, and the boundaries of scope. This volume is the fixed reference against which every subsequent volume is evaluated.

## Scope
Identity and intent only. Product modules, architecture, engineering, and go-to-market strategy are defined in later volumes of the Master Blueprint.
