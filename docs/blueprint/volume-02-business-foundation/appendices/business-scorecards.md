## Purpose

This appendix provides balanced-scorecard-style templates that translate strategy into a concise set of measures across complementary perspectives. It gives readers a ready structure for defining metrics, targets, and ownership so that performance is monitored in a balanced way rather than through financial results alone.

## Scope

The scorecards follow the four classic perspectives - Financial, Customer, Internal Process, and Learning and Growth. Templates include example metrics, targets, and frequency columns. Metrics shown are illustrative starting points to be replaced with organization-specific measures.

## How to Use

1. Confirm the strategic objectives the scorecard must support.
2. For each perspective, select a small number of measures that best evidence progress.
3. Set a target and threshold for each measure and assign an owner.
4. Define the measurement frequency and data source.
5. Review actuals against targets on a fixed cadence and act on gaps.

## Financial Perspective

| Objective | Metric | Target | Frequency | Owner |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Grow revenue | Revenue growth (YoY) | +15% | Monthly | Finance Lead |
| Improve profitability | Gross margin | >= 60% | Monthly | Finance Lead |
| Protect liquidity | Operating cash flow | Positive each month | Monthly | Finance Lead |
| Increase efficiency | Operating expense ratio | <= 35% of revenue | Quarterly | Finance Lead |

## Customer Perspective

| Objective | Metric | Target | Frequency | Owner |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Increase satisfaction | Customer Satisfaction (CSAT) | >= 90% | Monthly | Customer Lead |
| Build loyalty | Net Promoter Score (NPS) | >= 50 | Quarterly | Customer Lead |
| Retain customers | Customer retention rate | >= 92% | Monthly | Customer Lead |
| Acquire efficiently | Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC) | <= target ratio to LTV | Monthly | Growth Lead |

## Internal Process Perspective

| Objective | Metric | Target | Frequency | Owner |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Deliver reliably | On-time delivery rate | >= 95% | Weekly | Operations Lead |
| Ensure quality | First-pass quality rate | >= 98% | Weekly | Quality Lead |
| Improve throughput | Average cycle time | -10% vs. baseline | Monthly | Operations Lead |
| Meet commitments | SLA compliance | >= 99% | Monthly | Operations Lead |

## Learning and Growth Perspective

| Objective | Metric | Target | Frequency | Owner |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Build capability | Training completion rate | >= 95% | Quarterly | People Lead |
| Retain talent | Voluntary attrition rate | <= 8% annually | Quarterly | People Lead |
| Engage people | Employee engagement score | >= 80% | Semi-annual | People Lead |
| Drive improvement | Improvement actions closed | >= 90% of planned | Quarterly | Improvement Lead |
