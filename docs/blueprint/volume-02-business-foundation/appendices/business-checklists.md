## Purpose

This appendix provides actionable, reusable checklists for the recurring operational moments referenced across Volume 02. Each checklist reduces reliance on memory, enforces consistency, and creates a verifiable record that critical steps were completed.

## Scope

The checklists cover general business operations: launching a new process, closing a period, gating quality, verifying compliance, and making go/no-go decisions. They are function-neutral and intended to be copied and adapted. Copy a list and check items as they are completed.

## Checklist 1 - New Process Launch

- [ ] Process purpose and expected outcome documented
- [ ] Process owner and accountable role assigned
- [ ] Inputs, outputs, and handoffs mapped end to end
- [ ] Standard operating procedure written and reviewed
- [ ] Roles and responsibilities agreed (RACI complete)
- [ ] Required tools, access, and templates provisioned
- [ ] Success metrics and targets defined
- [ ] Affected people trained and briefed
- [ ] Pilot run completed and issues resolved
- [ ] Rollback or contingency plan in place
- [ ] Go-live approved by process owner
- [ ] Post-launch review scheduled

## Checklist 2 - Month-End Review

- [ ] All transactions for the period recorded
- [ ] Accounts reconciled (bank, receivables, payables)
- [ ] Revenue recognized correctly for the period
- [ ] Expenses accrued and cut-off applied
- [ ] Profit and loss statement prepared and reviewed
- [ ] Cash flow position confirmed
- [ ] Key metrics compared to plan and prior period
- [ ] Material variances explained
- [ ] Outstanding action items from prior month checked
- [ ] Results shared with relevant stakeholders
- [ ] Period formally closed and locked

## Checklist 3 - Quality Gate

- [ ] Requirements clearly defined and testable
- [ ] Deliverable measured against acceptance criteria
- [ ] Defects logged, triaged, and prioritized
- [ ] Critical and high-severity defects resolved
- [ ] Peer or independent review completed
- [ ] Standards and conventions verified
- [ ] Documentation updated to match deliverable
- [ ] Sign-off obtained from the accountable role

## Checklist 4 - Compliance Check

- [ ] Applicable laws, regulations, and standards identified
- [ ] Required policies and controls in place
- [ ] Evidence of adherence collected and retained
- [ ] Data handling meets privacy and retention rules
- [ ] Required approvals and authorizations obtained
- [ ] Third-party and supplier obligations verified
- [ ] Gaps or exceptions documented with remediation plan
- [ ] Compliance status reported to the responsible owner

## Checklist 5 - Go / No-Go Decision

- [ ] Decision objective and success criteria stated
- [ ] Readiness of all workstreams confirmed
- [ ] Open risks and issues reviewed with mitigations
- [ ] Dependencies and prerequisites satisfied
- [ ] Resource and capacity availability confirmed
- [ ] Financial and legal implications reviewed
- [ ] Rollback / contingency plan validated
- [ ] Stakeholders consulted and aligned
- [ ] Decision authority present to approve
- [ ] Go / No-Go decision recorded with rationale
