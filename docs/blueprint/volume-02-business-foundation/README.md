## Purpose
Establish the business knowledge layer for Project WORLD. This volume defines, from first principles, the business concepts on which every future AI module, ERP module, workflow, analytics engine, recommendation engine, and decision engine will rely. It is written as an enterprise business architecture manual and is the authoritative reference for business terminology and models across the platform.

## Scope
General business architecture knowledge organized into eight sections and a set of appendices. This volume defines concepts, models, relationships, and reference frameworks. It does not define WORLD's own commercial strategy, pricing, or market plans; those belong to later Product Management volumes.

## How to Use This Volume
Each chapter is a standalone reference document with consistent metadata and cross-references. Concepts are defined from first principles, illustrated with tables, diagrams (Mermaid), and examples, and related to how WORLD will operationalize them. Terminology is consistent across the entire volume and aligned with [Volume 01 Key Definitions](/docs/blueprint/volume-01-vision-and-philosophy/10-key-definitions.md).
