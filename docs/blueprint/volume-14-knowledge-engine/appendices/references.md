## Purpose

This appendix collects the established, real-world works, concepts, and standards on which Volume 14 draws. Its purpose is intellectual honesty and traceability: WORLD's Knowledge Engine is not invented in a vacuum but builds on decades of work in information retrieval, knowledge representation, and data management. Naming those sources lets a reader verify the reasoning, go deeper, and understand which ideas WORLD has adopted, adapted, or deliberately extended.

## Scope

The document lists foundational references grouped by topic, covering retrieval-augmented generation, embeddings and vector search, knowledge graphs, ontologies and semantic web standards, the knowledge hierarchy, and metadata and data-management standards. Each entry names the work and its author or origin; formal citation details can be resolved from these names through their publishers or the originating organizations. To preserve accuracy, this appendix does not fabricate URLs or invent bibliographic identifiers. It is a curated starting point, not an exhaustive bibliography.
