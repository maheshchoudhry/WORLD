## Purpose
Define the complete Knowledge Engine architecture of Project WORLD: knowledge philosophy, lifecycle, graph and registry, knowledge sources, retrieval and context, structure and semantics, quality and governance, and the enterprise knowledge platform. The Knowledge Engine is the memory and understanding layer that grounds the AI Business Partner (Volume 03) and AI Agents (Volume 13), draws from the ERP (Volumes 05-06) and Database (Volume 09), and feeds Business Intelligence & Analytics (Volume 04).

## Scope
Knowledge foundations; knowledge sources (documents, policies, SOPs, business rules, research); retrieval and context (context engine, retrieval, semantic search, embeddings, vector strategy); structure and semantics (relationships, ontology, taxonomy, metadata); quality and governance; and platform and evolution, plus appendices. This volume realizes the Knowledge Engine set out in Volume 08 (ch 17) and the knowledge subsystem of Volume 03.

## How to Use This Volume
Each chapter explains its Purpose, Architecture, Data Flow, Relationship with AI, Relationship with ERP, Relationship with Analytics, and Implementation Strategy. Chapters use Mermaid diagrams, tables, examples, and cross-references, and maintain consistent terminology aligned with Volumes 03, 04, and 09.
