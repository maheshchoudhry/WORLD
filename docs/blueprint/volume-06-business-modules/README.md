## Purpose
Provide complete enterprise documentation for every business module that runs on the WORLD ERP Foundation (Volume 05). Each module is specified as a functional building block of the AI-native operating system, with consistent structure across purpose, process, data, rules, workflow, KPIs, roles, and AI features.

## Scope
Module-level functional documentation for 32 business modules organized into eight domain sections, plus practical appendices. Module specifications build on the ERP Foundation (Volume 05), operationalize the Business Foundation (Volume 02), are reasoned over by the AI Business Partner (Volume 03), and feed Business Intelligence (Volume 04). Detailed physical schemas belong to Volume 09 (Database).

## How to Use This Volume
Each module chapter follows an identical enterprise template: Purpose, Business Value, Objectives, Responsibilities, Business Process, Master Data, Transactions, Business Rules, Workflow, Inputs, Outputs, Dependencies, KPIs, Reports, Dashboards, Roles, Permissions, AI Features, and Future Expansion. Chapters use tables, Mermaid diagrams, and examples, cross-reference Volumes 02/03/04/05, and maintain consistent terminology.
